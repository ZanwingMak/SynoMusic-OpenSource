SynoMusic Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

本隐私政策说明 SynoMusic 如何在 App 内处理你的服务器配置、媒体资料、播放数据、购买状态和第三方服务请求。

中文

1. 我们的原则

SynoMusic 是一个用于个人音乐库的播放器。我们不运营云端音乐账号服务，不出售你的个人信息，也不会主动收集你的 NAS 密码、音乐文件或播放内容到我们自己的服务器。

2. App 会在本机处理的信息

为了提供功能，SynoMusic 可能会在你的设备上保存或处理以下信息：服务器地址、QuickConnect ID、端口、用户名、服务器备注、登录状态、本地歌单、收藏、播放历史、下载记录、歌词设置、主题偏好、播放器设置、播客订阅、本地导入歌曲以及缓存文件。

服务器密码保存在 iOS Keychain 中。下载歌曲和本地导入文件保存在 App 沙盒中。删除 App 通常会移除本机 App 数据，但 Keychain 或 iCloud 中的部分数据可能需要通过系统设置或重新安装后的 App 功能单独处理。

3. NAS 与媒体库访问

当你添加自己的 Synology NAS 或兼容 Audio Station 服务时，App 会直接向你填写的服务器地址发送登录、资料库、播放、歌词、封面、下载等请求。你的 NAS、网络服务商或反向代理可能会记录这些访问。请只连接你有权访问的服务器和媒体内容。

4. iCloud 同步

如果你开启 iCloud 同步，SynoMusic 会通过 Apple iCloud 同步部分配置，例如服务器地址、用户名、本地歌单、主题和外观偏好。密码不会写入 iCloud 同步数据。iCloud 数据由 Apple 按其服务条款和隐私政策处理。

5. 内购与订阅

SynoMusic 的订阅、试用、终身解锁、恢复购买和兑换由 Apple App Store / StoreKit 处理。我们不会收到你的完整支付卡号或账单资料。App 只会读取 StoreKit 返回的商品信息、购买状态和权益状态，用于解锁相应功能。

6. 第三方服务

部分功能会请求第三方服务，例如 Apple Podcasts / iTunes 搜索和公开 RSS、Radio-Browser、LRCLIB、封面搜索或外部网页搜索入口（如 Google、百度、维基百科、萌娘百科）。当你使用这些功能时，查询关键词、播客或电台请求、IP 地址和设备网络信息可能由对应第三方服务处理。第三方服务的隐私政策适用于其处理行为。

7. 支持与反馈

如果你通过 GitHub Issues、电子邮件或 App Store 评论联系开发者，你主动提供的内容可能包括问题描述、设备信息、系统版本、截图、日志片段或联系方式。请不要公开提交 NAS 地址、账号、密码、令牌或其他敏感信息。

8. 数据删除与控制

你可以在 App 内删除服务器配置、下载内容、播放历史、本地歌单、播客订阅或本地导入文件。你也可以在 iOS 设置中删除 App 或管理 iCloud 数据。订阅取消、退款和购买记录由 Apple 管理。

9. 儿童隐私

SynoMusic 不面向儿童提供专门服务，也不会有意收集儿童个人信息。如果你认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请通过支持入口联系。

10. 安全

我们使用 iOS Keychain、App 沙盒和系统权限来保护本机数据。但任何网络传输和软件系统都不可能保证绝对安全。请使用可信网络、HTTPS、强密码和必要的 NAS 安全设置。

11. 政策更新

我们可能会随功能、法律或审核要求更新本政策。更新后的版本会发布在本页面，并调整生效日期。

12. 联系方式

如需支持、反馈或隐私相关请求，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系：https://github.com/ZanwingMak/SynoMusic-OpenSource/issues

English

1. Our approach

SynoMusic is a player for your personal music library. We do not operate a cloud music account service, we do not sell your personal information, and we do not intentionally collect your NAS password, music files, or listening content on servers operated by us.

2. Information processed on your device

To provide app features, SynoMusic may store or process the following information on your device: server address, QuickConnect ID, port, username, server label, login state, local playlists, favorites, playback history, download records, lyric settings, theme preferences, player settings, podcast subscriptions, local imported songs, and cached files.

Server passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain. Downloaded songs and local imports are stored in the app sandbox. Deleting the app usually removes local app data, but some Keychain or iCloud data may need to be managed through system settings or app features after reinstalling.

3. NAS and media library access

When you add your own Synology NAS or compatible Audio Station service, the app sends login, library, playback, lyrics, artwork, and download requests directly to the server address you provide. Your NAS, internet provider, or reverse proxy may log those requests. Only connect to servers and media content you are authorized to access.

4. iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, SynoMusic may sync selected configuration through Apple iCloud, such as server addresses, usernames, local playlists, theme, and appearance preferences. Passwords are not included in iCloud sync data. iCloud data is handled by Apple under Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

5. In-app purchases and subscriptions

Subscriptions, trials, lifetime unlocks, purchase restoration, and offer code redemption are handled by Apple App Store / StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card number or billing details. The app only reads product information, purchase state, and entitlement state returned by StoreKit to unlock app features.

6. Third-party services

Some features request third-party services, such as Apple Podcasts / iTunes Search and public RSS feeds, Radio-Browser, LRCLIB, artwork lookup, or external web search links such as Google, Baidu, Wikipedia, and Moegirlpedia. When you use these features, query terms, podcast or radio requests, IP address, and device network information may be processed by those third-party services. Their own privacy policies apply.

7. Support and feedback

If you contact the developer through GitHub Issues, email, or App Store reviews, the content you choose to provide may include issue descriptions, device information, system version, screenshots, log snippets, or contact information. Please do not publicly submit NAS addresses, account names, passwords, tokens, or other sensitive information.

8. Data deletion and controls

You can delete server profiles, downloads, playback history, local playlists, podcast subscriptions, and local imports in the app. You can also delete the app from iOS settings or manage iCloud data through Apple settings. Subscription cancellation, refunds, and purchase history are managed by Apple.

9. Children’s privacy

SynoMusic is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us through the support link.

10. Security

We use iOS Keychain, the app sandbox, and system permissions to protect local data. However, no network transmission or software system can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure. Please use trusted networks, HTTPS, strong passwords, and appropriate NAS security settings.

11. Changes

We may update this policy as features, laws, or review requirements change. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

12. Contact

For support, feedback, or privacy requests, please contact us through GitHub Issues: https://github.com/ZanwingMak/SynoMusic-OpenSource/issues
